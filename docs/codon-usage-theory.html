Alternative codon usage bias encodings

The RSCU is not the only method to summarize the codon usage bias of an organism. Hughes AL and Langley KJ proposed an alternative representation that encodes codon usage bias into five variables and this approach was succesfully applied to metagenomic binning by Yu G et al.
